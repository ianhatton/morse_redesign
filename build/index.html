Our Approach

  • "Seek first to understand": We take a brief then work out what the client requires (not always what the client thinks they require).
  • Propose a solution in non-tech terms: We like to speak Plain English. Drawing pictures helps, too.
  • Build a Prototype: If there's something innovative or particularly high-tech in the proposed solution, we build a demo before the client commits wholesale to the project. Our prototype-to-full-build ratio is 100% so far.
  • Agree a plan: Little explanation needed here...
  • Deliver. On budget: We have a little rule we call our "No Surprises" rule. We work to a fixed cost for a fixed project. If the client asks for more, we agree any extra charges in advance. Our clients never receive an invoice they're not expecting.

Who Hires Us?

Our clients include:

  • Client-facing Agencies who need client-presentable technical experts
  • Agencies who have sold a client an idea which they have no idea how to deliver
