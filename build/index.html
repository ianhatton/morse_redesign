What's a "System"?

In the digital world, a system means much more than just a website which is an online brochure for a business. Although we do build lots of those, our real expertise is in building:

  • Dynamic Database-driven Websites
  • Management Systems
  • Communication Systems
  • Extranets
  • Automated Comercial Processes
  • eCommerce
  • iPhone and iPad apps.

Who Hires Us?

Our clients include:

  • Media Owners

  • Design Agencies who need digital build capabilities
  • Client-facing Agencies who need client-presentable technical experts
  • Agencies who have sold a client an idea which they have no idea how to deliver
